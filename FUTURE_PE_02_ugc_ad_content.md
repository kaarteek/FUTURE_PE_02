## SECTION 5 — BRAND VOICE QUICK GUIDE

| Do This ✅ | Avoid This ❌ |
|------------|--------------|
| Sound like a real person talking | Sound like a formal advertisement |
| Use local words naturally — amma, dabba, ghar | Over-explain what the product does |
| Mention Hyderabad specifically | Use vague terms like "Indian food" |
| Talk about feelings — comfort, savings, home | Just list features without emotion |
| Keep hooks short and direct | Start with "We are a company that..." |
